## How to Use

### Getting Started
1. Open the app in your browser
2. Start with Week 1 or select any week from the dropdown
3. Review the Spanish word and try to recall its meaning
4. Click "Show Translation" or press Space to reveal the answer

### Learning Workflow
1. **Study the word**: Read the Spanish word and try to remember its meaning
2. **Reveal translation**: See the English translation and example usage
3. **Mark your knowledge**:
   - Click "I Know This" if you're confident with the word
   - Click "Need More Practice" if you need to review it more
4. **Continue**: Move to the next word automatically or use navigation

### Keyboard Shortcuts
- **Space**: Reveal translation or move to next word
- **Left Arrow**: Previous word
- **Right Arrow**: Next word

### Progress System
- **Green border**: Words you've marked as "Known"
- **Yellow border**: Words you need to study more
- **No border**: Words you haven't reviewed yet

## Learning Strategy

### Week Structure
Each week focuses on thematically related vocabulary:
- **Week 1-2**: Essential grammar words (articles, pronouns, basic verbs)
- **Week 3**: Family, numbers, basic adjectives
- **Week 4+**: Expanding to everyday topics like food, body parts, clothing, etc.

### Recommended Approach
1. **Daily practice**: Spend 15-20 minutes per day
2. **Review previous weeks**: Regularly revisit earlier vocabulary
3. **Use the shuffle feature**: Mix up word order to avoid memorizing sequences
4. **Focus on context**: Pay attention to example sentences
5. **Track your progress**: Use the statistics to identify areas needing work

## Vocabulary Sources

The vocabulary list is curated from frequency analysis of Spanish text corpora, focusing on:
- Most commonly used words in everyday Spanish
- Essential grammar and function words
- High-frequency nouns, verbs, and adjectives
- Words that provide maximum communicative value

## Learning Goals

By completing this 27-week program, you will:
- Understand approximately 85-90% of common Spanish text
- Have a solid foundation for Spanish conversation
- Know the essential vocabulary for travel, work, and daily life
- Be prepared for intermediate Spanish learning materials
